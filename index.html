10 Free Recipes

Crispy Chicken Parmesan
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs + ½ cup Parmesan
  • 1 cup marinara + 4 oz mozzarella
  1. Dredge, fry 3–4 min/side.
  2. Top with sauce & cheese.
  3. Bake 10 min at 400°F.
Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry
  • ½ lb beef strips
  • 2 cups broccoli + 3 tbsp sauce
  1. Stir-fry beef 2 min.
  2. Add broccoli + water 3 min.
  3. Thicken sauce. Serve.

  2. Add beans & spices.
  3. Simmer 25 min.
Spiced Beef Tacos

Cinnamon Apple Crisp

  3. Bake 35 min at 375°F.

Classic Beef Stroganoff

  1. Sauté beef.
  2. Add sauce.
  3. Serve over noodles.

Classic Tiramisu
  • 2 egg yolks + ¼ cup honey
  • 8 oz mascarpone + ½ cup cream
  • 24 ladyfingers + 1 cup espresso

  3. Layer with espresso-dipped fingers. Chill 4 hrs.
