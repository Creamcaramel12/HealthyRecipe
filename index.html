Chicken Tikka Masala
  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt, 1 onion, 2 tomatoes
  • Spices: garam masala, turmeric, cumin
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt & spices 1 hr.
  2. Sauté onion, add tomato puree, spices.
  3. Add chicken, simmer 15 min. Serve with rice.
Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry
  • ½ lb beef strips, 2 cups broccoli
  • Soy sauce, garlic, ginger, cornstarch
  1. Brown beef 2 min, set aside.
  2. Stir-fry broccoli & garlic 3 min.
  3. Add sauce, return beef, thicken 1 min.
Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken
  • 4 chicken thighs, lemon, garlic, rosemary
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  1. Rub chicken with oil, herbs, lemon juice.
  2. Roast at 400°F for 35 min.
  3. Rest 5 min before serving.
Hearty Beef Chili
  • 1 lb ground beef, 1 can kidney beans
  • Onion, tomato paste, chili powder
  1. Brown beef & onion.
  2. Add spices, tomatoes, beans; simmer 30 min.
  3. Top with cheese & serve.
Creamy Chicken Alfredo
  • 8 oz fettuccine, 2 chicken breasts
  • Heavy cream, parmesan, garlic
  1. Cook pasta. Sauté chicken.
  2. Make cream sauce with garlic & cheese.
  3. Toss pasta & chicken in sauce.
Spiced Beef Tacos
  • 1 lb ground beef, taco shells
  • Cumin, paprika, onion, lettuce, cheese
  1. Brown beef with spices.
  2. Fill warm shells with beef & toppings.
  3. Serve immediately.
Garlic Butter Chicken
  • 4 chicken thighs, butter, garlic
  • Parsley, lemon, salt
  1. Sear chicken in butter 5 min/side.
  2. Add garlic, bast 2 min.
  3. Finish with parsley & lemon.
Molten Chocolate Lava Cake
  • 4 oz dark chocolate, ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs, ¼ cup sugar, 2 tbsp flour
  1. Melt chocolate & butter.
  2. Whisk eggs & sugar, fold in chocolate & flour.
  3. Bake in ramekins at 425°F for 12 min.
Cinnamon Apple Crisp
  • 4 apples, oats, brown sugar
  • Butter, cinnamon, flour
  1. Slice apples, toss with cinnamon.
  2. Mix oats, sugar, butter for topping.
  3. Bake 375°F for 35 min.
Silky Vanilla Panna Cotta
  • 2 cups heavy cream, ½ cup milk
  • ⅓ cup sugar, 1 vanilla bean
  • 2 tsp gelatin
  1. Heat cream, milk, sugar, vanilla.
  2. Bloom gelatin, stir in.
  3. Pour into molds, chill 4 hrs.
