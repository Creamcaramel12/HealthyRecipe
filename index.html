Chicken Tikka Masala

Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

Garlic Butter Chicken
  • 4 bone-in chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp parsley + 1 tsp lemon juice
  1. Season chicken. Sear in hot butter 5 min per side.
  2. Add garlic; baste chicken 2 min until golden.
  3. Stir in parsley and lemon. Serve hot.

Cinnamon Apple Crisp
  • 4 apples, peeled & sliced
  • ½ cup oats + ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup flour
  • ¼ cup butter + 1 tsp cinnamon
  1. Layer apples in dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  2. Mix oats, sugar, flour, butter into crumbs.
  3. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35 min until golden.
Silky Chocolate Mousse

  • 1 cup heavy cream, 2 tbsp sugar
  1. Melt chocolate. Whip cream to soft peaks.
  2. Beat yolks into chocolate, fold in whites.
  3. Fold in cream. Chill 2 hrs.

Classic Tiramisu
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 cup cooled espresso
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting)
  1. Whisk egg yolks and sweetener until pale and thick.
  2. Fold in mascarpone until smooth.
  3. Whip cream to soft peaks; fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers briefly in espresso; layer in dish.
  6. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers.
  7. Repeat layers. Chill 4 hours.
  8. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Triple Chocolate Mousse
  • 4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 4 oz white chocolate, chopped
  • 2 cups heavy cream, divided
  • 2–3 tbsp natural sweetener
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
    1. Melt each chocolate type separately; let cool.
    2. Whip 1½ cups of cream to soft peaks, fold in melted chocolates individually for layers.
    3. Add sweetener + vanilla to remaining ½ cup cream, whip slightly.
    4. Layer mousses in serving glasses or bowls (dark, milk, white); chill 3 hrs.
Crispy Chicken Parmesan
  • 2 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (preferably panko)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 4 oz mozzarella, sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  1. Season chicken; dredge in flour, dip in eggs, coat with breadcrumbs + Parmesan.
  2. Heat oil in skillet; fry chicken until golden, 3–4 min per side.
  3. Transfer to baking dish, top with marinara + mozzarella.
  4. Bake 10–12 min at 400°F (200°C) until cheese melts.
